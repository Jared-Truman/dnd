* Cantrips

** Spare the Dying                                             :phb:cantrip:
Necromancy cantrip

Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous

You touch a living creature that has 0 hit points. The creature becomes stable.
This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
